Cookie statement

Alliancys uses cookies on its website and several different techniques such as JavaScript and web beacons. We want to ensure your privacy on our website and improve usability. Therefore, we try to inform you of the use of these techniques.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small file that is sent along with pages of this website and stored by your browser on the hard drive of your computer. The information it contains will then be sent back to our servers on your next visit. By making use of cookies we ensure you of the following: Making it easier and more pleasant for you to navigate our website During a visit your session preferences will be stored, so you do not have to re-enter the same information every time you visit the website. You will not receive the same information every time you visit the website. We will measure the usability of our website to improve if necessary. We can offer you personalized advertisements and/or recommendations based on your interests We can recognize the device you are using when you visit our website.

Browser settings

If you do not want have website cookies on your computer you can adjust your browser settings. You will receive a warning before the cookies will be saved. Furthermore, you can adjust the browser settings in such way that all cookies or only the third-party cookies will be rejected. We would like to draw your attention to the fact that we cannot guarantee that our website will work properly if you do not accept the cookies. It could appear that certain functions on the website do not work anymore or that they are no longer visible. Rejecting cookies does not mean you will not see advertisements, only that they are not adjusted to your interests. Adjusting your browser settings is different per device and browser.
